Piano Practices

▶

コウを追いかけて — from the film Drowning Love

A haunting, cinematic theme steeped in longing, turbulence, and raw emotion.
Its melody moves with the restless energy of chasing something just out of reach.
It echoes the idea that “humans cannot fly, but we can run,”
capturing the desperate, grounded yearning at the heart of the story
and the reckless beauty of youth pulled toward desire.

Love 119 — RIIZE

A bright and energetic track from the Korean boy group RIIZE.
It glows with the warmth of a winter first crush—
fresh, innocent, and heart-fluttering.
The melody rushes forward with excitement,
like the spark of falling for someone for the very first time.

明明就 — Jay Chou

A lighthearted love song filled with sweetness and youthful sincerity.
The tune carries a playful, nostalgic charm,
capturing the shy emotions of first affection
and the soft honesty that defines young love.

▶

Always Online — JJ Lin

A soft, sentimental ballad woven with warmth and quiet loneliness.
Its melody lingers like a message left unread—
a gentle reminder that presence can exist even in silence,
and that some connections stay with us no matter the distance.
